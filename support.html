Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How can I protect my content?

A: You can upload your content through the "Upload & Consent" section. Once uploaded, you can set permissions to control how your content is used and distributed.

Q: What should I do if someone downloads my content without permission?

A: If your content has been downloaded without your consent, you will receive a notification in the "Notifications" section. You can take action by contacting our support team for further assistance.

Q: Can I track who is using my content?

A: Yes! Our platform allows you to track the usage of your content. You will be notified whenever your content is accessed, shared, or downloaded.
