Consent Management

By agreeing to this, you grant permission for your content to be shared or used according to the specified access types (Public/Private). You can revoke this permission at any time.

Your consent ensures that your content is protected under our blockchain system. Only authorized users will have access to your content based on the permissions you specify. Any unauthorized usage will trigger notifications, keeping you informed about the use of your content.

Additionally, you can track how your content is being used, who has accessed it, and if it's been downloaded or shared without your consent. This feature ensures that your intellectual property is always safeguarded.
